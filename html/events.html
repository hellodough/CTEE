Events

WOStock

WO-stock is a two day music festival with over 60 bands playing. It takes place in West Ottawa High School and supports local music. All profit goes towards shipping computers to Cameroon and other stuff.

Stuff the Bus

Stuff the Bus is a canned food drive in front of local grocery stores during the holidays. Students ask the community to donate canned foods and put them on a bus to send to the community action house to help families in need.

Snowball

Snowball is a winter formal dance at West Ottawa High School. Students get jiggy with it and dance off that cabin fever.
